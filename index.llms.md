# Shambhala Mirror standards

Background

## 1 History

The Societal Mirror is a 5-year effort to support the Shambhala Community’s ability to look at itself – as a community and an organization.

## 2 Opportunity

The [https://mirror.shambhala.org](Shambhala%20Societal%20Mirror) has evolved incrementally over the years. This standards site is an opportunity to look at its current state and describe it in a way that accelerates the next phase of development. The goal is to create a set of standards that can be used to guide future development.

## 3 What’s included

- DRAFT website standards
- Industry standards from mature Newsrooms
- Some website metrics

## 4 Possible futures

- Standards for processing open-ended comments
- Survey standards
